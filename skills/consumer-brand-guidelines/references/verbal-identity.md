# Verbal Identity

Source: Zillow April 2024 Brand Guidelines, slides 23-35.

## Brand Story

Few things are more powerful than home. Home provides physical and emotional security for today, and financial opportunity for tomorrow. But for many, finding and getting home is incredibly fraught, marked by complexity at best, and real barriers at worst.

When Zillow began, access to housing data was held by the industry, giving it the advantage, and leaving everyday people in the dark. So we turned on the lights, creating a level of transparency that empowered the dream of home.

We believe that home should be more than just a dream. Our purpose is to **make home a reality for more and more people**.

## Personality Traits

| Trait | Meaning | Expressed As |
|-------|---------|-------------|
| **Courageous** | Bravely push boundaries and stand up for what's right | Stands up for what's right · Pushes boundaries · Innovates with purpose |
| **Insightful** | Deeply understands context; leverages expertise to create unique insights | Understands context · Provides unique insight · Creates clarity |
| **Unflappable** | Unwavering commitment through adversity; consistently delivers | Unwavering through adversity · Provides emotional stability · Consistently delivers |

## Archetype: The Advocate

When writing or speaking on behalf of the Zillow brand, invoke the voice of an **Advocate**. Ask: "Is this something an advocate would or would not say?"

## Writing Principles

All writing principles should be applied all the time. "Show empathy" is the guiding principle that steers how all others are applied.

| Principle | Maps to Pillar | Maps to Personality |
|-----------|---------------|---------------------|
| **Show empathy** (guiding) | All | All |
| **Create possibility** | Possibility | Courageous |
| **Build trust** | Understanding | Insightful |
| **Inspire confidence** | Progress | Unflappable |

## Tone Values

Tone changes to reflect the situation, the audience, how they're feeling, and what they're trying to achieve. "Thoughtful" is the guiding tone that influences all others.

| Tone | Role | When to Use |
|------|------|-------------|
| **Thoughtful** (guiding) | Ever-present | Always; guides how other tones are applied |
| **Enthusiastic** | Energy | Celebrating milestones, new features, exciting moments |
| **Playful** | Delight | Light moments, exploration, browsing |
| **Direct** | Clarity | Functional content, instructions, forms, errors |
| **Empowering** | Confidence | Decision moments, financial tools, closing |
