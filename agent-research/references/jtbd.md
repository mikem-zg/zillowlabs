# Jobs To Be Done — By Role and Phase

Complete JTBD framework for real estate agents, organized by role (listing agent, buyer's agent) and transaction phase. Use when designing features that map to specific agent workflows.

## Contents
- Listing Agent JTBDs (by phase)
- Buyer's Agent JTBDs (by phase)
- Shared JTBDs (both roles)
- Cross-Persona JTBD Summary

## Listing Agent (Sellside Agent)

### Prospect Phase
- **Nurture leads and relationships** — maintain sphere of influence, stay top-of-mind with past clients and referral sources
- **Track leads** — monitor lead status, source, and engagement across channels
- **Qualify and prioritize leads** — identify serious sellers vs. tire-kickers; allocate time to highest-potential opportunities
- **Market myself and my services** — build personal brand, create content, maintain online presence, generate inbound interest

### Earn Client Business Phase
- **Determine seller's commitment** — assess motivation, timeline, and readiness to list
- **Win the seller's listing** — deliver compelling listing presentation, differentiate from competing agents, establish trust and expertise

### List the Home Phase
- **Strategize how to best market the home** — determine pricing strategy, staging, photography, and marketing channels
- **Make seller's home appealing to potential buyers** — coordinate staging, photography, virtual tours, floor plans
- **Publish the listing** — enter listing data into MLS, syndicate to portals, ensure accuracy and compliance
- **Generate interest in the home** — drive showings through marketing, open houses, broker events, social media

### Select an Offer Phase
- **Advise seller using market and buyer feedback** — synthesize showing feedback, market data, and competitive intel to guide pricing and strategy adjustments
- **Evaluate and select the best offer** — analyze offer terms (price, contingencies, timeline, financing), present options clearly, guide seller decision

### Transact Phase
- **Coordinate and resolve transaction logistics** — manage inspections, appraisals, repairs, title, escrow, and closing timeline
- **Guide seller through emotions** — manage expectations, reduce anxiety, maintain confidence through surprises and setbacks

### Post-Transact Phase
- **Nurture the client relationship after closing** — closing gifts, drip campaigns, market updates, referral requests; drive repeat and referral business

### Manage My Business (Ongoing)
- **Get paid** — manage commission splits, track closings, handle disbursements
- **Maintain professional knowledge and licensing** — continuing education, certifications, compliance
- **Evaluate and improve my performance** — track metrics (deals, volume, conversion, CSAT), identify improvement areas
- **Manage day-to-day activities** — calendar, task management, prioritization
- **Integrate into a new brokerage or team** — onboard to new systems, relationships, and workflows
- **Manage business operations** — accounting, tax, insurance, legal, office management
- **Shape business strategy** — set goals, define target markets, plan growth
- **Stay up to date on industry** — regulatory changes, market trends, technology shifts
- **Stay up to date on local market** — neighborhood trends, comparable sales, development activity

## Buyer's Agent (Buyside Agent)

### Prospect Phase
- **Nurture leads and relationships** — maintain sphere, stay top-of-mind, cultivate referral sources
- **Track leads** — monitor buyer lead status, source, and engagement
- **Qualify and prioritize leads** — assess readiness, motivation, and financial capacity
- **Market myself and my services** — build brand, create content, maintain presence

### Earn Client Business Phase
- **Win the buyer's commitment** — establish trust, demonstrate value, secure buyer-broker agreement
- **Assess the buyer's financial readiness** — understand budget, pre-approval status, financing options

### Find the Home Phase
- **Match buyers with the right homes** — search MLS, set up alerts, curate property lists based on criteria
- **Guide buyer through tradeoffs** — help prioritize needs vs wants, educate on market conditions, manage expectations

### Secure the Home Phase
- **Advise buyer during negotiation** — strategy on offer price, terms, contingencies, escalation
- **Guide buyer through emotions** — manage anxiety, excitement, and fear during high-stakes decisions
- **Assemble a winning offer** — coordinate pre-approval letters, earnest money, offer terms, and submission

### Transact Phase
- **Coordinate and resolve transaction logistics** — manage inspections, appraisal, repairs, title, escrow, closing
- **Ensure buyers secure funding** — coordinate with lender, track loan milestones, resolve issues

### Post-Transact Phase
- **Nurture the client relationship after closing** — closing gifts, home anniversary reminders, market updates, referral cultivation

## Shared JTBDs (Both Listing and Buyer's Agents)

- **Get paid** — commission tracking, split management, disbursement
- **Manage day-to-day activities** — calendar, tasks, prioritization
- **Maintain professional knowledge and licensing** — CE, certifications, compliance
- **Integrate into a new brokerage or team** — onboarding, system setup, relationship building
- **Evaluate and improve my performance** — metrics tracking, self-assessment, coaching
- **Shape business strategy** — goal setting, market selection, growth planning
- **Manage business operations** — accounting, tax, insurance, legal
- **Stay up to date on industry** — regulatory, market trends, technology
- **Stay up to date on local market** — comps, neighborhood trends, development
